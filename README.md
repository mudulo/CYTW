# Curb Your Timewasting

**Available for Chrome and Firefox.**

![Screenshot of Curb Your Timewasting](https://github.com/mudulo/CYTW/raw/master/screenshot.png)

To avoid wasting time on Twitter and YouTube, this extension asks you why you are visiting the sites and then loads a search results page of the site with this reason or query.
